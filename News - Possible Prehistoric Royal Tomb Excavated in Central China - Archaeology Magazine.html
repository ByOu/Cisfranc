Credit card Discovered in Pyramids

News October 25, 2024

SHARE:
  • Share to Facebook
  • Share to X
  • Share via Email
  • Copy permalink to clipboard Copied to clipboard

Le Caire, Egypt. Archeologists report that the credit card of a person named Luc Eygasier was found in a pyramid. Apparently the credit card would be 2000 years old. We can read on a tablet close to the credit card "osti je l'avais déjà sorti y'a 2000 ans la carte"
